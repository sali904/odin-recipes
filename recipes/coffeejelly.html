The Disastrous Life of Saiki K's Coffee Jelly

Kusuo Saiki's Favorite Dessset: Coffee Jelly

The Disastrous Life of Saiki K is slice of life comedy anime that follows the wacky life of Kusuo Saiki, a psychic teenager, as he navigates high school. Although he tries to live a mundane life despite his powers, he still gets caught off guard by the shenanigans of his family and (reluctantly-made) friends. How does he manage to keep his cool despite his constant yet reluctant involvement in his peers' shenanigans, you ask? Coffee jelly.

Coffee jelly is exactly what it sounds like - jellied coffee. It is made using a few simple ingredients: coffee, gelatin, sugar, and some sort of creamy topping - whipped cream, sweetened condensed milk, sweet cream, or even ice cream. Overall, this recipe, courtesy of Funimation, is easy to make and definitely enjoyable, especially if you enjoy coffee. Try this recipe today!

Ingredients

  • Coffee
  • Unflavored gelatin
  • Sugar
  • A creamy topping of your choice. Suggestions: whipped cream, sweet cream, ice cream, condensed milk (Kusuo tends to have his coffee jelly with whipped cream on top).
  • Dessert cups

Steps

  1. Dissolve the gelatin by following the directions on the gelatin mix.
  2. Heat coffee in a medium saucepan until it begins to boil slightly.
  3. Add dissolved gelatin and sugar until everything is mixed.
  4. Pour mixture into dessert cups.
  5. Place cups in refrigerator and let chill for about 4 hours, or until jelly is completely firm and cool to touch.
  6. Once jelly is fully chilled and firm, add your creamy topping of choice and enjoy!
